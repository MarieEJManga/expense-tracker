Target Group: Students

This tool is built for University Students who want to manage their monthly allowance, rent, and social expenses without the hassle of heavy apps or spreadsheets.

How We Attract Users

  • University Networks: Promotion via student Discord servers and campus subreddits.
  • Open Source Learning: Encouraging CS students to contribute to the code for their own portfolios.
  • Quick Start: Preset categories for student life (Tuition, WG-Rent, Coffee, Books).
